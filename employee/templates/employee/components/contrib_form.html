Guidelines

Case Study

Case Study is a detailed analysis of a case or a check that you have worked on. It must include complete information around the following:

  • What was the scenario?
  • What were the challenges associated with it?
  • What were the steps (in sequence) involved in getting the case completed?
  • What were the extra measures or initiatives required, if any, to complete the case?
  • What did you learn from the entire process of completing the case?
Essential Training

Essential training is a significant piece of information that is necessary for performing the day to day tasks. It must include complete information around the following:

  • What is the information?
  • Which part of your process does it impact or is relevant to?
  • What are the examples?
